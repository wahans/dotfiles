Goal: automate meeting note taking, synthesis, and organization for CrossLayer meetings.

**Relevant folders (all in `~/cl/knowledge/`):**
- `granola-notes/` — exported meeting notes from Granola app (md/docx/txt formats), with export process documented
- `vault/` — meetings archive and knowledge vault
- `meeting-prep/` — liquid manager questions framework, prep script, PDFs

**Why:** These three are intentionally related — the workflow is: Granola captures → notes export → synthesis/organization → vault storage.

**How to apply:** When working on meeting note automation or synthesis for CrossLayer, check all three dirs together. Any automation should connect Granola exports → cl-vault.
